# Risk Assessment – Sample Organization

## Asset
Customer database containing PII

## Threats
- Unauthorized access
- Data exfiltration
- Insider misuse

## Vulnerabilities
- Weak access controls
- No MFA
- Lack of monitoring

## Risk Impact
High – potential regulatory fines and reputational damage

## Risk Mitigation
- Implement MFA
- Apply least privilege
- Enable audit logging
