## Organization Overview
A mid-sized SaaS company processes customer PII and relies on multiple third-party vendors.

## Objectives
- Identify key risks
- Evaluate existing controls
- Recommend improvements aligned to NIST CSF

## Key Risks Identified
1. Excessive user permissions
2. Limited vendor oversight
3. Inconsistent access reviews

## Existing Controls
- Access Control Policy
- Vendor risk questionnaire
- Incident response plan

## Gaps Identified
- No documented access review cadence
- Vendor assessments not risk-tiered

## Recommendations
- Implement quarterly access reviews
- Introduce vendor risk scoring
- Map controls to NIST CSF

## Outcome
This review improved visibility into organizational risk and supported compliance readiness.
