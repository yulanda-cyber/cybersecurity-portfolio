# Access Control Policy

## Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to define requirements for controlling access to organizational systems and data to reduce the risk of unauthorized access.

## Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties with access to company systems and data.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Users must safeguard their credentials
- Managers approve access requests
- IT/Security enforces access controls

## Access Requirements
- Access is granted based on the principle of least privilege
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for remote and privileged access
- Access reviews are conducted quarterly

## Account Management
- Accounts are disabled after 90 days of inactivity
- Access is revoked immediately upon termination

## Enforcement
Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
